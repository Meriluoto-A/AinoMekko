****AinoMekko**** is a fictional Nordic fashion brand operating in luxury, premium and standard apparel market. The company sells dresses andshoes through several sales channels and operates internationally.

🏬****Sales channels:****
  + Online store
  + Flagship stores: Tokyo, Paris, New York
  + Retail partners: Finland, Estonia
  + Design licensing to selected external brands

🏭****Production sites:****
 + Lisbon
 + Tallin
 + Chicago

❓***Research Questions:***
+ What are the top-performing products by revenue, sales volume and gross margin?
+ Do luxury product outperform standard products in terms of margin?
+ Can the customer base be clustered to suprt more targeted marketing strategies?
+ How can logistics routes from production sites to flagship stores be optimized to: reduces transport distance, minimize CO2 emissions
+ What happens to margins and profitability if production or logistics costs increase 25%

**Skills Demonstrated:**'
+ pandas for data analysis
+ logistics route optimization using pandas
+ customer segentation and clustering
+ data visualization by Seaborn
+ power BI dashboard for executive decision-making support
